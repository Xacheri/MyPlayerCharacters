**Type**: Hazard  
**Environment**: Dark, damp locations  
**Size**: 5-foot square and larger patches

### Effects

If disturbed, yellow mold releases a **cloud of spores** in a **10-foot radius** around itself. Creatures caught in the spore cloud suffer the following effects:

- **Damage**: Creatures take **11 (2d10) poison damage**.
- **Saving Throw**: Creatures must make a **DC 15 Constitution saving throw**.
    - **On a failed save**: The creature is **poisoned** for 1 minute and takes an additional **5 (1d10) poison damage** at the start of each of their turns.
    - **On a successful save**: The effect ends.
- **Repeating the save**: The creature can repeat the Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the poisoned effect on itself on a success.
### Vulnerabilities

- **Fire damage**: Instantly destroys yellow mold.
- **Sunlight**: Instantly destroys yellow mold.
