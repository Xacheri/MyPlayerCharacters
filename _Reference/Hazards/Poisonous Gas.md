Any creature that enters poisonous gas for the first time on a turn or starts a turn in poisonous gas makes a DC 12 Constitution saving throw, taking 1d10 poison damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. Creatures in the gas also have disadvantage on Death Saving Throws.

***Scaling for Higher Levels***: Use the following table to scale the poison gas damage for higher levels.

| **Levels** | **Poison Damage** | Save DC |
| ---------- | ----------------- | ------- |
| 5-10       | 2d10              | 14      |
| 11-16      | 4d10              | 16      |
| 17-20      | 10d10             | 18      |
