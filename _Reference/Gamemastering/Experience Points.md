Player characters advance in level by earning experience points (XP) through adventures, defeating foes, and completing quests. When a character's XP total meets or exceeds the threshold for the next level, they level up, gaining new abilities and features according to their class progression.

- **Cumulative XP**: The total experience required to *reach* that level.
- **XP to Next Level**: The amount of XP needed to advance from that level to the next.


## Experience Points Per Encounter

A game session will typically consist of 2-4 *enocunters*. An encounter could be a combat, but ultimately an encounter is any challenge that the players overcome. Experience points should be planned out based on the overall difficulty of the encounter and the level of the players involved. 

For combat encounters, use the XP Budget per Character to establish a budget to spend on enemies for the encounter. For non-combat encounters, reward characters based on the encounter difficulty after-the-fact.
